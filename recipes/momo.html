momo

Description

This is a simple momo recipe.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 3/4 cup water (adjust as needed)
  • 1 tablespoon oil (optional)
  • 1 cup finely chopped vegetables or meat for filling
  • 1 teaspoon soy sauce
  • 1 teaspoon ginger-garlic paste
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the all-purpose flour and salt.
  2. Gradually add water and knead the dough until it becomes soft and pliable. You can also add oil for extra softness.
  3. Cover the dough with a damp cloth and let it rest for at least 30 minutes.
  4. Prepare the filling by mixing the chopped vegetables or meat with soy sauce, ginger-garlic paste, salt, and pepper.
  5. Divide the dough into small balls (about the size of a golf ball).
  6. Roll out each ball into a thin circle using a rolling pin.
  7. Place a spoonful of filling in the center of each circle and fold the edges to seal the momo. You can pleat the edges for a traditional look.
  8. Steam the momos in a steamer for about 10-15 minutes until they are cooked through.
  9. Serve hot with your favorite dipping sauce.
