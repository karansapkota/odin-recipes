roti

Description

This is a simple roti recipe.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups whole wheat flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 3/4 cup water (adjust as needed)
  • 1 tablespoon oil (optional)

Steps

  1. In a large mixing bowl, combine the whole wheat flour and salt.
  2. Gradually add water and knead the dough until it becomes soft and pliable. You can also add oil for extra softness.
  3. Cover the dough with a damp cloth and let it rest for at least 30 minutes.
  4. Divide the dough into small balls (about the size of a golf ball).
  5. Roll out each ball into a thin circle using a rolling pin.
  6. Heat a skillet or tawa over medium-high heat. Place the rolled-out roti on the skillet.
  7. Cook for about 1-2 minutes on one side until you see bubbles forming, then flip it over and cook for another 1-2 minutes.
  8. Press gently with a spatula to help it puff up. Flip again if needed until both sides have golden brown spots.
  9. Remove from the skillet and keep warm in a clean kitchen towel. Repeat with the remaining dough balls.
